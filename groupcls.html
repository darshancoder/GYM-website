CROSSFIT GROUP TRAINING CLASS

WHAT IS CROSSFIT?

CrossFit is a high-intensity, strength, and conditioning workout that consists of a wide variety of
functional movements – many of which you would perform daily throughout your everyday life (e.g.,
pushing, pulling, squatting, sitting up, etc.)

CrossFit workouts include variations of these movements with a strong focus on load, distance,
and speed. Each exercise typically lasts a set period or until a certain amount of work has been completed.
CrossFit workouts are different each day, and Our Trainers can modify every exercise according to an
individual’s ability regardless of their age and fitness levels.

AERIAL YOGA CLASSES

Aerial Yoga is a hybrid exercise form comprising of traditional
yoga poses, pilates and dance. It makes use of a silk
hammock, suspended over a yoga mat. It adds an interesting
challenge in completing complex asanas. It helps you burn body
fat while building toned and lean muscles. Want to achieve
a Zen state of mind? Then this one’s for you!

BENEFITS OF AERIAL YOGA

1. Strengthens core muscles
2.Increases blood flow and supply
3. Works in reducing migraines and improves breathing
4. Aids in improving posture
5. increase spinal flexibility and movement
5. Calms you and reduces stress
